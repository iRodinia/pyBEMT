Introduction:

- This is the explanation of the structure of airfoil.dat files in the "airfoils/" folder.

Structure:

 - Front 14 lines: Header, description of the airfoil. [Skipped while reading the data]

 - Airfoil data: 3 columns of data

    angle-of-attack (alpha) [in degrees],  lift coefficient (Cl),  drag coefficient (Cd)

    The Cl and Cd will be interpolated using interp1d in 'quadratic' mode.
